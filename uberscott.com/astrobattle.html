Bluray/Astro Battle

A 2D space shooter where players can build a space ship and then fight with it online
Enthusiastic fans of 2D space shooters all over the world!
Player can build a ship by snapping several "modules" together. The positioning and weight of the ship modules affects the game physics when playing online. Realtime multiplayer online action.
I programmed this game from scratch and hired people to create graphic art and 3D assets. The game was actually popular circa 2004-2006 but since then I haven't had time to work on it.
